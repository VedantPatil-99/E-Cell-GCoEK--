E-Cell

Government College of Engineering

Entrepreneurship Cell is a nurturing and progressive student-run organization that deals with minute aspects leading bright minds to create and build new ideas. Inculcating into startups or securing good jobs and thus reaching the pinnacle of success. We provide overall learning, gathering, and execution-based system to get a higher than ever student success ratio to our future graduates.

About Us

E-Cell, IIT Kanpur aims to induce an entrepreneurial mindset into the students and air an innovative streak in them. We are here to water the "Ideas" in the bud and help them bloom into impactful endeavors through networking student enterprises from campus to incubators, seeding funds and angel investors to transform the newly proposed ideas into successful start-ups.

Events

What We Do

E-Cell organises a variety of events throughout the year to promote entrepreneurial activities in and around the campus. Our annual E-Summit witnesses huge participation from all over India. Events like TEDx and StartUp Master Class take the learning to a whole new level. E-Cell also conducts various lectures and workshops focussed at budding entrepreneurs. StartUp Internship Programme helps IITK students land internships in start-ups and get to know their working in greater depth.

E-Summit

E-Summit is an annual flagship event of IIT Kanpur aimed at fostering entrepreneurial initiatives and activities on campus. It provides a platform for sharing entrepreneurial experiences and discussing ideas, helping participants nurture their entrepreneurial skills.

TEDx

TEDx IIT Kanpur is an independent initiative under TED, featuring a series of talks by eminent speakers in Technology, Entertainment, and Design. It aims to spread innovative ideas and inspire the campus community with diverse perspectives.

UpStart

UpStart is a unique event for budding startups, featuring multiple rounds of screening and face-to-face mentoring. It aims to provide participants with more than just prizes, focusing on idea development and business plan refinement through interactions with industry experts.

StartUp Master Class

In association with the Alumni Association, IIT Kanpur, the StartUp Master Class (SMC) is an enriching platform for entrepreneurs. It brings together startups, mentors, and investors to foster networking and collaboration within the entrepreneurial community.

Startup Internship Program

The Startup Internship Program offers IITK students opportunities to intern with various startups, helping them gain hands-on experience. The 2017-18 season saw a significant increase in internship offers, with startups like Invento Robotics, Ridlr, and Dunzo participating.

Campus Hangouts

Campus Hangouts are informal discussion sessions between students and entrepreneurial experts. These sessions provide a platform for brainstorming and discussing various aspects of entrepreneurship, fostering a vibrant and interactive learning environment.
